No Passwords

No password is required to sign in to your calendar.

Instead, a temporary, random code is sent to your email each time you need to log in (which shouldn't be that frequently if you're using Planned On to its fullest capacity).

This way, you don't have to remember a stupid password or any other nonsense to get in to your calendar.

And, as long as you're the only one with access to your email, it's more secure.

Passphrase

Set a custom passphrase to further secure your account. The longer, the better. You'll never have to enter this to sign in.
