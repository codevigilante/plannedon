Remember To Do All The Things

I created this thing is so I could remember all the things I care about doing, like writing, training, and building silly things like this. It also helps me remember boring stuff, like going to the dentist, or registering my stupid car. Planned On will help you remember, without being intrusive or restrictive.

Stay Organized

My life used to be a series of bad decisions and impulses. Since I started planning my days and weeks, I'm way more organized and get way more done. It feels good being in control, even if it's really an illusion. Planned On can help you get and stay organized, easily.

Procrastinate Less

"Less" because there's no way to eliminate it completely. There's just some things that we don't really want to do, like taking out the trash or listening to our significant other bitch about what a slob we've become. Planned On will help you limit extreme procrastination, and might even cure mild procrastination.

Because Traditional Calendar Apps Suck

They're stupid, cumbersome, inflexible, and full of features you don't need. And everything feels like a boring meeting you have to attend with a bunch of people you hate, and if you miss the meeting and do something fun instead, you feel guilty. Planned On frees you from this prison, allowing you to be, well, you and not some corporate drone.
